Privacy

Effective 22 June 2026 · tres.studio is operated by Tres Carter, Seagrove · 30A, Florida.

Short version: this is a personal portfolio. I collect a small amount of anonymous, aggregate data to understand how the site is used and to improve it. I don't run ads, I don't sell or share your data, and I don't try to identify you.

01What's collected

  • Umami analytics — privacy-first, cookieless analytics. It records aggregate visits, the pages viewed, referring links, and approximate country and device type. It does not use cookies, does not store your IP address, and cannot identify you or follow you across other sites.
  • Microsoft Clarity — heatmaps and anonymized session replays (clicks, scrolls, and cursor or touch movement) so I can see where the layout works and where it doesn't. Text you type into fields is masked. This data is processed by Microsoft on my behalf and may involve cookies.
  • Visitor counter — the "online now" and "total visits" numbers in the footer. These come from an anonymous count tied to a random id stored in your browser for the session only (not a cookie). It holds nothing that identifies you.

02Cookies

Umami sets no cookies. The visitor counter uses your browser's session storage, not a cookie. Microsoft Clarity may set its own analytics cookies. You can block or clear cookies anytime in your browser settings.

03Who processes it

  • Microsoft Clarity — heatmaps and session replay. See the Microsoft Privacy Statement.
  • Umami — analytics. See umami.is/privacy.
  • Netlify — hosting; standard server logs.
  • Cloudinary — image delivery.
  • Google Fonts — serves the typefaces; your IP is visible to Google when fonts load.

04Your choices & rights

You can browse with Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control enabled, block scripts or cookies, or opt out of Microsoft Clarity through your browser controls. If you're in the EU or UK (GDPR), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data relating to you, and object to its processing. If you're in California (CCPA/CPRA), note that I do not sell or share personal information. To exercise any of these, just email me.

05Retention, children & changes

Analytics are kept in aggregate; Clarity data follows Microsoft's retention schedule. This site isn't directed at children under 13. If this policy changes, the new version is posted here with an updated date.
